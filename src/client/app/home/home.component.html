Trustless

Bitcoineum runs as an Autonomous Decentralized Application responsible for generating coins through a synthetic mining process. Designed to be immune from human intervention and replacing flawed governence with immutability.

Decentralized

Running on top of the Ethereum blockchain as an ERC20 token provides seamless interoperability across a range of smart contract services. Users have control of coin creation, not entrenched mining interests.

Value

Bitcoineum emulates Bitcoin's semantics, preserves the 21 Million coin cap. A strong store of value with access to Ethereum's native smart contract ecosystem and interoperability across a wide range of on chain instruments. Brings Bitcoin capabilities into Ethereum, rather than strapping smart contracts onto Bitcoin.

It is Time for a change

Something is wrong in Crypto. It is becoming increasingly obvious that even a system with extensive checks and balances, and designed to withstand governments is ultimately vulnerable to human greed and manipulation. Bitcoineum changes this equation, and introduces a new digital gold standard.
